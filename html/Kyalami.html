Kyalami Corner

Johannesburg, South Africa

Kyalami Corner shopping centre, opened April 2017, perfectly responds to the retail, lifestyle and social requirements and aspirations of the Kyalami neighbourhood and surrounds. The centre is conveniently located off the R55 and Main Road, while the elegant, energy-efficient design complements the natural, equestrian environment and character of the area. The centre includes flagship Woolworths Food and Checkers stores, a 3 500m² Virgin Active gym and a choice of restaurants to impress even the gourmet foodie, including Jackson’s Real Food Market, The Baron, Pizza e Vino and Salsa Mexican Grill, to name a few. Kyalami residents now have a desirable, convenient, well-stocked ‘local’ that provides for all their everyday needs. Those who live in and outside the precinct make a point of visiting Kyalami Corner for the incredible variety, luxury and atmosphere the centre offers. Strategically positioned close to the Kyalami Race Track, at the busy intersection of the Kyalami main road and the R55 from Lonehill/Bryanston, Kyalami Corner offers easy access and ample free parking in this trendsetting precinct. The design of the buildings, which are located on the fringes of the equestrian zone, takes inspiration from surrounding environment. Working for, SVA International as Project Architect and Executive Associate we have gone to great lengths to ensure that the 28 000 m² centre’s design not only reflects the character of the area, but also incorporates the latest sustainable and energy sufficient practices. The use of natural materials such as wood, natural stone, corrugated metals and indigenous plants emphasise the architectural theme throughout.
